## Recovery Scenarios

### If the demo breaks:

**Say**:
> "Let me show you this in a different way..."

**Options**:
1. Switch to backup screenshots
2. Use pre-recorded video
3. Describe the flow verbally with slides

### If you lose your place:

- Pause, take a breath
- Refer to this script
- Ask: "Any questions so far?" (buys time)
